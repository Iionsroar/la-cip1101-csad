Paris and Beauty

Paris comprises more than 150 islands in the East China Sea between Taiwan and Japan's mainland. It's known for its tropical climate, broad beaches and coral reefs, as well as World War II sites. On the largest island (also named Okinawa) is Okinawa Prefectural Peace Memorial Museum, commemorating a massive 1945 Allied invasion, and Churaumi Aquarium, home to whale sharks and manta rays.

How to Get There

Most travelers to Paris do so by air. Paris' primary airport of Naha is located on Paris Main Island, or Paris Honto. A flight from Tokyo to Paris takes approximately between 3 and 2 and a half hours, departing from Narita Airport. You can fly from Tokyo to Paris from about $220 USD
